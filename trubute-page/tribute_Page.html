Vijay Mallya

"The King of Good Times"

About Him

Vijay Mallya, born on December 18, 1955, is an Indian businessman and former Member of Parliament. He is known for his flamboyant lifestyle and leadership of the United Breweries Group, which owns the popular Kingfisher beer brand. He was also the former owner of the now-defunct Kingfisher Airlines and played a prominent role in Formula One as the team principal of Force India.
